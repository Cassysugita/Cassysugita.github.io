Music

These three playlists are very special, each one is dedicated to someone in my life who was or is very important to me. Each one embodies a specific person and how I felt about them. Playlist number four coming soon!

My Playlists:

The Process of Love

Published on June 18, 2017,

was the first of it's kind. The order and the titles of the tracks walks you through the inevitable stages of youthful love. I made this playlist based on what I saw was happening everywhere around me, little did I know that I would later expirience it's exact contents myself. The person that this playlist is dedicated to taught me how to break out of my shell and showed me the world from a new perspective. The idea that I tried to get across is that love is not just said and done but instead a never ending cycle.

Genre: Lo-fi

Sixteen

Published on Feburary 17, 2019,

Sixteen was a birthday gift. It was the recievers 16th birthday and that is where this playlist got its title. Sixteen goes into the detail of a relationship, all the best parts and the occasional bad parts, as shown by track seven, Dead To Me. This was the only playlist that made it to a physical CD, the only copy ever made belongs to the person that this was dedicated to.
